# Reflection
This problem set covered several heat transfer concepts and involved analyzing different thermal systems. I chose this assignment because it felt very applicable to real engineering situations. It combined several heat transfer concepts including conduction, convection, and radiation, and helped illustrate how they interact within the same system. Working through the problems helped me see how these mechanisms can be modeled together using a resistance network, which made the overall heat transfer process much clearer. It also gave me experience analyzing conduction through multiple material layers, which is common in many real devices.

After completing this assignment, I feel much more comfortable breaking down a physical system into a thermal model and analyzing heat transfer through layered materials. Many engineering systems require careful thermal management, especially electronics or energy systems where overheating can affect performance. Knowing how to model these situations will be important for future engineering work for designing electronic components, energy devices like solar panels, or systems where temperature control is critical such as spacecraft or other advanced mechanical systems.
